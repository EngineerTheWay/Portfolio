About Me

I’m a former Air Force foreign language and cyber intelligence analyst transitioning into a career in tech. I'm passionate about using information systems to solve real-world problems and improve how organizations operate—especially in mission-driven environments.

With nearly a decade of experience in military intelligence, cyber operations, and classroom instruction, I’ve built a strong foundation in strategic thinking, problem-solving, and team leadership. I’ve led cross-functional projects that automated workflows, enhanced cybersecurity, and improved access to organizational resources.

As I pivot into the private sector, I’m targeting roles such as business analyst, systems analyst, or IT operations specialist—where I can bridge the gap between users, leadership, and technical teams. I’m excited to build expertise in enterprise IT, cloud platforms, and security while helping organizations achieve impact through smart, user-centered technology solutions.

Professional Objectives

I’m pursuing opportunities as a business analyst, systems analyst, or IT operations specialist—roles where I can leverage my background in leadership, operations, and digital transformation to improve how organizations work. I’m especially drawn to positions that bridge business needs with technical solutions, enabling teams to work more efficiently and make informed decisions.

Looking ahead, I plan to pursue a full-time MBA to strengthen my business acumen, expand my strategic skillset, and prepare for a long-term career at the intersection of business and technology. My goal is to help mission-driven organizations use technology more effectively to deliver impact, improve operations, and support long-term innovation.
